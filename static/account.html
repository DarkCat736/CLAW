All About CLAW (v0.1.1)

You are using a DEMO, WORK IN PROGRESS version of CLAW! Please note that there will be bugs, which you should report at https://github.com/DarkCat736/CLAW/issues.

CLAW is a platform for allowing students to collaborate and stay organized. In order to do this in the best way possible, your account data is stored in the cloud instead of on-device, meaning your organization tools are available from anywhere you can access the internet! In addition to cloud data storage, we also utilize APIs such as Socket.io to provide seamless, realtime collaboration experiences.

Please remember that we take student safety very seriously, and if abuse reports are made we always investigate.

Thank you and have a great time using CLAW!
